Automate Asset Sharing with Smart Contracts

Easily and Fairly Distribute Your Crypto Assets Among Multiple Addresses with ProRata Wallet's Customizable Smart Contracts

Features
Smart Contract-Based Sharing
ProRata Wallet uses smart contracts to allow users to easily share received assets with other addresses they specify. Users can set the percentage of assets to be shared with each address, and the smart contract automatically distributes the funds accordingly.
User-Friendly Interface
ProRata Wallet has a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for users to manage their assets and view their sharing settings. The web3-based platform allows users to interact with the wallet from any device with an internet connection.
Secure and Transparent
ProRata Wallet is built on the blockchain, ensuring the security and transparency of all transactions. The smart contract-based sharing system is tamper-proof, and users can verify all transactions on the blockchain. Additionally, ProRata Wallet does not store any private keys or personal information, providing users with full control over their assets.
How It Works
1
Connect Wallet
To connect your wallet to ProRata, simply click on the "Connect Wallet" button located at the top right corner of the page. You will then be prompted to select your preferred wallet provider, such as MetaMask or Trust Wallet. Once you have selected your wallet provider, a pop-up window will appear asking you to authorize the connection. After you have authorized the connection, your wallet will be linked to ProRata, and you will be able to view and manage your assets directly from the platform. Connecting your wallet to ProRata is a quick and easy process, allowing you to easily track and manage your assets with just a few clicks.
Create ProRata Wallet
To create a ProRata smart wallet, simply follow the steps on the app. First, ensure that you have connected your wallet to the app via Web3. Next, you can create a new ProRata smart wallet. Set the share percentage for each address that will receive a portion of the assets, and the smart contract will automatically manage the distribution of the assets based on the defined percentages. The ProRata smart wallet is a secure and transparent way to manage asset sharing with others, without the need for manual tracking or management.

Share ProRata Address
Once you have created your ProRata wallet, you can easily share the wallet address with others who want to contribute to the asset pool. When someone sends assets to the shared ProRata wallet address, the smart contract automatically distributes those assets to the specified addresses based on the percentage of shares defined for each address. This ensures a transparent and fair distribution of assets among all contributors. By sharing the ProRata wallet address, you can easily collaborate with others and pool your assets together to achieve common goals, whether it's investing in a project or managing a joint expense.
